Studio vision

Vitae Interactive as a future studio

Vitae Interactive isn’t just a portfolio label — it’s the early shape of an independent studio focused on playful systems, approachable tools, and games that quietly teach you how they work while you’re having fun.

Mission & creative focus

Long term, I imagine Vitae Interactive as a small team crafting:

  • Mechanics-driven games where movement and abilities feel expressive.
  • Readable systems with clear feedback and low “design friction.”
  • Thoughtful difficulty curves that invite curiosity instead of frustration.

The prototypes and tools on this site are the first pieces of that future studio’s tech and design backbone.

Play · Build · Teach

The studio idea lives on three pillars:

  • Play – small but polished experiments, released on itch.io.
  • Build – internal tools, frameworks, and reusable systems.
  • Teach – breakdowns, tutorials, and assets other devs can plug in.

You can already see this starting on vitae-interactive.itch.io , where future experiments will live.
