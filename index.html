Studio vision

Vitae Interactive as a future game studio

Vitae Interactive is more than a personal portfolio name — it’s the seed of a future independent studio focused on tight-feeling gameplay, readable systems, and projects that teach as they entertain.

Mission & creative focus

The long-term goal for Vitae Interactive is to grow into a small studio that builds mechanics-driven games with:

  • Expressive systems – combat, movement, and abilities that feel great to use.
  • Readable design – clear feedback, strong affordances, and low “design friction”.
  • Intentional difficulty curves – challenge that grows with the player, not against them.

Early prototypes and tools I build now are meant to become the tech backbone of future Vitae Interactive games.

Studio pillars & future offerings

As Vitae Interactive evolves, the studio is imagined around three core pillars:

  • Play – systems-first games with responsive controls and rich player expression.
  • Build – internal tools and workflows that keep iteration fast and designers unblocked.
  • Teach – sharing breakdowns, tutorials, and assets so other devs can learn from each project.

In the near future, Vitae Interactive will ship itch.io prototypes, system-focused asset packs, and technical design tutorials alongside more ambitious game projects.
